Video Streaming

In this demo, you will see how you can stream MP4 videos with MPEG-DASH format. Click here to know more about Streaming.

Basic Video Streaming

Source Code:

                        
                            <video width="1000" height="600" data-dashjs-player="" autoplay controls loop>
                                <source src="~/content/output/finalsamplevid.mpd" type="application/dash+xml">
                                Your browser does not support the video tag.
                            </video>

Video Streaming with Subtitles

Source Code:

                        
                            <video width="1000" height="600" data-dashjs-player="" autoplay controls>
                                <source src="~/content/outputwithsubtitles/stream.mpd" type="application/dash+xml">
                                Your browser does not support the video tag.
                            </video>
